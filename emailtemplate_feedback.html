New Year's Eve wishes from the Data Team!

Haiti Now is a data-driven organization. We use data as critical evidence to help inform and influence our strategy and also to help formulate solutions for the Restavek problem.

The Data Team at Haiti Now came into existence about a year ago. It started with the goal of learning more about the Restavek situation from data collected by reputable* institutions. It wasn’t exactly easy to form this team of equally enthusiastic like-minded people who were pulsating to come up with visualizations (or vizzes - that’s how we address our creations☺) which would depict the truth of this exploited section of Haiti. Once the team started building up under Alex and the able leadership of Gauri, there was simply no looking back!
